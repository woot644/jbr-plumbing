Your Current Website

Issue Found Why It Matters
Hosted on free Replit subdomain — no custom .com.au domain Google ranks custom domains higher. Free subdomains signal low trust to customers.
Mobile performance score: 61/100 Google penalises slow mobile sites. 7.2-second load time means most visitors leave before the page loads.
First Contentful Paint: 6.0 seconds Should be under 1.8s. Visitors see a blank screen for 6 seconds.
Largest Contentful Paint: 7.2 seconds Core Web Vital. Google considers this "Poor" — directly hurts rankings.
Broken/hotlinked stock images from Pixabay Images can break without warning. Not unique content.
No schema markup (LocalBusiness, Service) Missing from Google rich results — competitors with schema get more clicks.
Only 257 words of content Google sees thin content as low-value. Competitors have 1,000+ words.
No Google Analytics tracking No way to measure visitors, enquiries, or what's working.
No sitemap.xml or robots.txt Google can't efficiently discover and index your pages.
Personal Gmail as business email Looks unprofessional — customers trust business domain emails.
"Made with Replit" footer Signals the site was built with a free tool — undermines trust.
Hourly rate displayed publicly Can undercut your value before customers even enquire.
